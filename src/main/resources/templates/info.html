Survival Guide

Everything you need to know for JPrime 2026

Sofia Tech Park

Location: Sofia, Bulgaria • Sofia Tech Park.

Registration Basement of the main Amphitheater building. Open from 8:00 AM.
Hall B (Tent) Located in the middle of the village square. Always comfortable but bring a layer if it's breezy!
Workshops Located in the Incubator building right next door.

Parking Guide

FREE PARKING (Recommended):

Park on the opposite side of boulevard "Tsarigradsko Shose" for absolutely free. Walk across using the pedestrian bridge. Easy!

PAID PARKING:

Available inside Sofia Tech Park. Rates are approximately 1 BGN per hour (or more, organizers don't set the prices).

Food & Refreshments

You won't go hungry! Catering is fully included with your ticket:

  • ☕ First Breakfast: Coffee, croissants, and snacks waiting at registration (8:00 AM).
  • 🥪 Second Breakfast: Refreshments between early morning sessions.
  • 🍔 Lunch & Beer: Full lunch provided. Drink responsibly!

Hackathon & Raffle

This year is special:

  • 🏆 Hackathon: €1,000 first prize for the best app. Coding sessions run through both days.
  • 🎁 Raffle: At the end of Day 1. Win cool prizes from sponsors! Check in to get entered.
